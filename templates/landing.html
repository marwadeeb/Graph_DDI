How It Works

From your question to a clinical answer in milliseconds

1

Enter Two Drug Names

Type any drug name, brand name, or DrugBank ID. The system auto-completes from 4,795 approved drugs and resolves synonyms automatically.

2

Instant Two-Stage Check

First, we search 824,000 documented DrugBank pairs in milliseconds. If the pair is novel, our Graph Neural Network predicts an interaction from pharmacological patterns.

3

Clear, Sourced Result

Every answer tells you exactly where it came from — documented evidence from DrugBank, or an AI prediction — with a confidence score and a safety note.

⚕️ For reference only. This tool is designed to support clinical decision-making, not replace it. Always consult a qualified pharmacist or physician before changing any medication regimen. Documented interactions are sourced from DrugBank v5.1; novel predictions are generated by a machine learning model and have not been individually validated.
