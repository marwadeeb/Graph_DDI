How It Works

From query to clinical insight in milliseconds

1

Enter Drug Names

Type any drug name or DrugBank ID. Autocomplete resolves brand names and synonyms across 4,795 approved drugs.

2

Two-Stage Lookup

O(1) dictionary check against 824K DrugBank pairs. If no match, the HeteroGraphSAGE GNN predicts interactions from pharmacological graph patterns.

3

Transparent Result

Every result is clearly sourced — documented (DrugBank) or predicted (GNN) — with confidence scores, evidence passages, and safety guidance.
