Graph heuristics (Adamic-Adar, CN, Jaccard) score 0 for any node pair where neither node appears in the training graph — i.e. cold-start drugs. They require no training but can only leverage topology. Logistic Regression uses 212 node features (physicochemical, ATC codes, CYP450 profiles) concatenated as symmetric pair vectors — it works for cold-start but ignores graph structure. The GNN combines both.
